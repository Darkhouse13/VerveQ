Daily Football Quiz

Every day, VerveQ serves ten football questions — the same ten for every player in the world. You get one attempt. Answer fast for bonus points, then share your score and see who among your mates actually knows their football.

Play today's quiz — free

How it works

  • 10 questions, one attempt per day. No retries — what you score is what you share.
  • Same questions for everyone. Your 8/10 and your mate's 6/10 are directly comparable.
  • Speed matters. Up to 100 points per question, decaying the longer you think.
  • Streaks. Complete the daily to keep your streak alive; miss a day and it resets.
  • Resets at midnight UTC. A fresh quiz every single day.

During the World Cup: the daily runs as a special World Cup edition — every question themed to the tournament, all tournament long.

Why one attempt?

Because that's what makes the score mean something. Anyone can get 10/10 with retries. The daily quiz is a level playing field: same questions, same clock, one shot. That's why players share their results — a score you can't grind is a score worth bragging about.
