How it works

  • Async, not appointment TV. You play now; they play when they open the link. No scheduling.
  • Same questions, seeded per duel. Fair fight — nobody gets the easy set.
  • No account needed to accept. Your mate taps the link and plays as a guest, straight away.
  • Rivalry ledger. Every duel updates your head-to-head record — W-L-D, streaks, receipts.
  • Share cards that unfurl. Duel invites preview with a taunt card in WhatsApp and iMessage, not a bare link.

The rematch rule: the loser always demands one. That's the point — one duel is a game, a rivalry ledger is a habit.
